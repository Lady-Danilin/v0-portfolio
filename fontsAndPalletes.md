🎯 Font Strategy Tips:

Headlines: Use bold, impactful fonts (Bebas Neue, Oswald)
Body Text: Prioritize readability (Inter, Source Sans Pro)
CTAs: Match your palette's energy level
Data/Metrics: Clean, technical fonts (Inter, Roboto)
Brand Names: Custom or distinctive fonts (Montserrat, Playfair)
